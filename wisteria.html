Insignia

Psychological Assessment

Pleasant on the outside, the viper is all blissful smiles and happy chatters, disarming people with an almost childlike sincerity. She is playful, happy, chipper and chatty, and will chatter away with strangers on a whim without much friction… that is, unless she starts finding someone a little *too* interesting.

Beneath the surface of her playful and chipper facade, lies something deeply flawed. While normally she is agreeable and easy to approach, once she finds someone of her interest, she will latch onto them, trying to find out what buttons to push to get them to break. She does not feel empathy towards people, watching people with the same idle fascination a child would with a trapped insect- like children would poke and prod an insect to watch it squirm, she breaks people, to see what spills- grief, fear, screams and desperation.

She is drawn to intense emotions, like moth to a fire, like grimm to negativity.

Combat Data

Weapons: Fangs

A dual dagger-revolver hybrid.

Although the weapon technically has two forms, she rarely uses the revolver forms as her combat style dictates that she stay consistently in her enemy’s space, negating the need for ranged combat. She will however collapse her daggers into their revolver forms if she cannot close distance, or simply feels the want to.

The Dual daggers are each named “Hush” and “Venom” after the two drugs the Viper Den supplies to Remnant. In dagger form, they’re regular old daggers, with a chamber that holds Dust- Hush holds ice dust, and Venom holds lightning dust within their individual chambers.

When collapsed into their respective revolver forms, they can fire 6 rounds each before needing to be reloaded. She carries spare ammunition to reload with, though she rarely makes use of them.

The revolver forms make use of the Dust chamber as well, and with a click of a button, the ammunition is imbued with their respective dusts to fire ice/lightning rounds at her opponent, depending on which weapon she fires from.

Dust Used:

Ice Dust

Lightning Dust

Both Lightning and Ice dust are used in her revolver, usually meant to stall her enemies long enough for her to close distance so that she can engage in close-range combat.

Fire Dust

In powdered form, placed inside a vial. She carries three vials with her. When needed, she tosses the vial and sets it off to cause a moderate explosion at the site.

Range: Close-quarters specialist

Notable Traits: Prehensile tail, high mobility, psychological pressure

Threat Level: Extreme

Semblance: Tainted Aura

Her semblance is “Tainted Aura”, that is injected through a bite into her enemies. Her aura, once in the enemy’s system, will linger and cause intense, debilitating neurological pain in the target, that can only be quelled by having Wisteria stabilize her Aura that has taken root within them. The pain is immense, most average person becoming desperate for relief- relief that only Wisteria or time provides.

Her aura fades inside the infected target over time, disappearing completely from the system in approximately two weeks. However, every time Wisteria manually stabilizes her aura within the target, the two week timer is reset. A person with enough pain tolerance and willpower can fight through two weeks to be free of her semblance, or potentially become dependent on her for relief.

Her semblance cannot be used on enemies with their aura shield intact, and can only infect people post-combat after the targets aura shield has already been broken. This makes her semblance non-viable for combat use, and it’s mostly used to control people she has already bested in battle… Or simply for her amusement.

Each bite costs 30% of her aura, and has a hard limit of 3 bites a day, and cannot be used even if her aura has recharged past 30%. She can bite three times consecutively, however it leaves her with miniscule Aura for protection, so she’s more likely to spread her bites out throughout the day.

She can only have 15 people infected at once, and to infect new people with her tainted aura, she’d have to drop someone already infected.

Recovered Background File

Early Home Life

Wisteria was born into a household ruled by addiction, debt, and violence. Her father gambled away their possessions while her mother drowned herself in alcohol. The chaos never seemed to frighten Wisteria. If anything, she listened to it with quiet interest.

By sixteen, the screaming, fighting, and breaking had become normal to her. When her father was taken by debt collectors, Wisteria watched her mother collapse beneath the grief — and something inside her awakened.

The First Poisoning

Knowing she and her mother would be taken next, Wisteria gathered poisonous flowers from the mountains: oleander, belladonna, lily of the valley, and aconitum.

She returned home with a decorated bottle of liquor and offered it to her mother as she always had. When the poison took effect, Wisteria watched with fascination rather than horror.

“Ah… there you are, mama.”

Rise of the Viper

After leaving home, Wisteria eventually found Aluran and planted him within the same organization that had threatened her family. One by one, members began disappearing.

Rumors spread of bloodied alleys, shrill laughter, revenge, justice, or karma. To Wisteria, it was simpler: entertainment. She discovered that people with more to lose broke more beautifully.

Claiming the Den

Wisteria strangled the organization from the outside until fear hollowed it out. When it was finally weak enough, she claimed its throne for herself.

She took Aluran under her wing with the promise of subjects, shelter, and companionship. Together, they built the Crime Den, profiting from the drugs known as Hush and Venom while expanding their influence through the underbellies of Remnant.
