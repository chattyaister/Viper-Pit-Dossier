WISTERIA “VIPER”

Subject File 001 — Organization Leader

← Return to Archive

Identity

Alias: Viper

Age: 31

Height: 5'4"

Weight: 130 pounds (+200 pounds tail)

Status: Active Threat

Role: Head of Organization

Combat Data

Weapons: Dual daggers / revolvers

Range: Close-quarters specialist

Notable Traits: Prehensile tail, high mobility, psychological pressure

Threat Level: Extreme

Semblance: Tainted Aura

Her semblance is “Tainted Aura”, that is injected through a bite into her enemies. Her aura, once in the enemy’s system, will linger and cause intense, debilitating neurological pain in the target, that can only be quelled by having Wisteria stabilize her Aura that has taken root within them. The pain is immense, most average person becoming desperate for relief- relief that only Wisteria or time provides.

Her aura fades inside the infected target over time, disappearing completely from the system in approximately two weeks. However, every time Wisteria manually stabilizes her aura within the target, the two week timer is reset. A person with enough pain tolerance and willpower can fight through two weeks to be free of her semblance, or potentially become dependent on her for relief.

Her semblance cannot be used on enemies with their aura shield intact, and can only infect people post-combat after the targets aura shield has already been broken. This makes her semblance non-viable for combat use, and it’s mostly used to control people she has already bested in battle… Or simply for her amusement.

Each bite costs 30% of her aura, and has a hard limit of 3 bites a day, and cannot be used even if her aura has recharged past 30%. She can bite three times consecutively, however it leaves her with miniscule Aura for protection, so she’s more likely to spread her bites out throughout the day.

She can only have 15 people infected at once, and to infect new people with her tainted aura, she’d have to drop someone already infected.

Psychological Assessment

Pleasant on the outside, the viper is all blissful smiles and happy chatters, disarming people with an almost childlike sincerity. She is playful, happy, chipper and chatty, and will chatter away with strangers on a whim without much friction… that is, unless she starts finding someone a little *too* interesting.

Beneath the surface of her playful and chipper facade, lies something deeply flawed. While normally she is agreeable and easy to approach, once she finds someone of her interest, she will latch onto them, trying to find out what buttons to push to get them to break. She does not feel empathy towards people, watching people with the same idle fascination a child would with a trapped insect- like children would poke and prod an insect to watch it squirm, she breaks people, to see what spills- grief, fear, screams and desperation.

She is drawn to intense emotions, like moth to a fire, like grimm to negativity.

Subject is manipulative, theatrical, and unusually calm during close-range confrontations.

Field Warning

Do not engage alone. Do not allow close contact. If the subject smiles during combat, assume the situation has already worsened.
