Organization Files

Organization Overview

It existed for 30 years, under different leaders and under the name of the Vermillion Pact. The organization was overthrown and taken over by Wisteria 10 or so years ago, and expanded rapidly from there. A rapidly growing syndicate situated in Mistral, the Viper Pit specializes in the production of their major drugs, “Venom”, and “Hush”, created after a long period of questionable experimentation carried out by the co-founder, Aluran

Primary Assets

The criminal organization deals mostly in their specialized drug “Venom” and “Hush” as well as information, most notably information of huntsmen and huntresses, students of huntsmen/huntress schools and villains alike.

VENOM HUSH

Known Activities

Rules

1. Do not cross the Viper.

2. Do not cross the Doctor.

3. Do not steal from the Pit. Take the food from the Viper’s maw and get bitten.

4. Resources are to be used, not squandered. Do not be wasteful.

5. Do not harm another member of the Viper Pit without authorization

6. Loyalty is expected, competence is rewarded.

Betrayal is final

Rankings of the Viper Pit

1. The Viper - Founder/Leader

2. The Doctor - Founder/Leader

3. Fangs - Elite enforcers/Problem-solvers

4. Scales - Information brokers/Infiltrators

5. Coils - Managers of operations

6. Handlers - Smugglers handling sales and transport

7. Shedskins - Rising members within the ranks

8. Hatchlings - New recruits

9. The bitten - Those who have been bitten by the viper and under her control.
