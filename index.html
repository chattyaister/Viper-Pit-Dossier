Organization Files

Organization Overview

It existed for 30 years, under different leaders and under the name of the Vermillion Pact. The organization was overthrown and taken over by Wisteria 10 or so years ago, and expanded rapidly from there. A rapidly growing syndicate situated in Mistral, the Viper Pit specializes in the production of their major drugs, “Venom”, and “Hush”, created after a long period of questionable experimentation carried out by the co-founder, Aluran

Primary Assets

The criminal organization deals mostly in their specialized drug “Venom” and “Hush” as well as information, most notably information of huntsmen and huntresses, students of huntsmen/huntress schools and villains alike.
