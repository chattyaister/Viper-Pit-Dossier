CLASSIFIED RECORD

WISTERIA "THE VIPER"

Subject File 001-D — Combat Capabilities

← Return to Subject File

Combat Data

Range: Close-quarters specialist

As she dual-wields daggers, she fights up close and personal with her enemies, a rather in-your-face style of fighting. She consistently maintains hugging distance, digging uncomfortably into her enemy’s personal space. She does not know the term personal space, and will taunt you with nicks of her blade and attacks that almost do but don't quite land, grazing along her aura but not quite connecting. She loves the game of it all.

Notable Traits: Prehensile tail, high mobility, psychological pressure

Threat Level: Extreme

Fangs (Hush & Venom)

A dual dagger-revolver hybrid.

Although the weapon technically has two forms, she rarely uses the revolver forms as her combat style dictates that she stay consistently in her enemy’s space, negating the need for ranged combat. She will however collapse her daggers into their revolver forms if she cannot close distance, or simply feels the want to.

The Dual daggers are each named “Hush” and “Venom” after the two drugs the Viper Den supplies to Remnant. In dagger form, they’re regular old daggers, with a chamber that holds Dust- Hush holds ice dust, and Venom holds lightning dust within their individual chambers.

When collapsed into their respective revolver forms, they can fire 6 rounds each before needing to be reloaded. She carries spare ammunition to reload with, though she rarely makes use of them.

The revolver forms make use of the Dust chamber as well, and with a click of a button, the ammunition is imbued with their respective dusts to fire ice/lightning rounds at her opponent, depending on which weapon she fires from.

Dust Usage

Ice Dust

Lightning Dust

Both Lightning and Ice dust are used in her revolver, usually meant to stall her enemies long enough for her to close distance so that she can engage in close-range combat.

Fire Dust

In powdered form, placed inside a vial. She carries three vials with her. When needed, she tosses the vial and sets it off to cause a moderate explosion at the site.

Strenghts

Melee combat. She has a preference for melee combat, and will fight up close and personal if given the chance, using her agility to her advantage to taunt her foes and counter.

Agility. She is flexible and agile, making her a rather frustrating foe to engage battle with, as she will avoid and avoid incoming attacks.

Improv. With her quick wit, she thinks up the strangest way out of tight situations, leaving people baffled and confused in her wake.

Indifference to pain She does not respond to pain like a normal faunus being, catching many off guard when she laughs through wounds and keeps coming at them with a smile on her face.

Weaknesses

Long range combat.Because of her preference for melee combat, she is lacking in long-ranged combat. If you can keep her at a distance, you have an advantage, though she knows this and will try to close distance every chance she gets.

Raw Strength.- The only part of her that is significant in strength is her tail. That thing is a powerhouse. The rest of her isn’t up to standards, however, and when tail is out of the equation, she’ll lose in raw strength to the average huntsman/huntress.

Recklessness. When in battle she is immensely reckless, happy to swap blows with enemies if it means she gets another hit, another wound into her enemy. She will fight blindly through her own injuries, critical or not.
