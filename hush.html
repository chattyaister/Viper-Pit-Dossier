HUSH

Concoction File 002

← Return to Subject File

VENOM

“Hush” comes in the form of a small teal crystal shaped like a drop, which is swallowed whole for an immediate effect.

Although rarely found within the well guarded large kingdoms, the drug Hush is commonly found in the outskirts of cities and in towns where grimm attacks are widespread and common, and lands are not well protected.

The crystal upon consumption suppresses the emotions of the consumer, leading to effects such as: 

             - Dampened panic responses
            
             - Reduction of grimm attraction. 
            
             - Quelled effects of PTSD and anxiety.
            
             - Numbness to pain

Users describe the sensation of taking hush as their thoughts growing distant and muffled, sounds becoming softer around the edges, bringing a quiet and calmness that they haven’t felt in a while. The drug suppresses the attraction of grimm, and when the user does engage in battle, allows the user to engage in combat in a calm, methodical manner.

Hush has the unintended and less than ideal effects of 

             - Emotional numbness with extended use 
             
             - Dependency/Addiction
            
             - Loss of empathy
            
             - Apathy
            
             - Indifference to danger

The Effect of Hush lasts approximately 8 hours after consumption.
